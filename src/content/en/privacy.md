### Contact by e-mail (“Ask a question”)

If you contact us by e-mail (e.g. via the “Ask a question” link), we process your e-mail address and the content of your message only to respond to you. Legal basis is our legitimate interest in answering enquiries (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) or, where applicable, pre-contractual or contractual steps (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR). We do not pass your data to third parties unless required by law. We retain correspondence as needed for follow-up and legal obligations.
